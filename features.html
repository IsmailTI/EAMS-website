Features
Asset Registry
This feature majorly deals with profiling your assets under;
  • Asset profile
  • Assignments
  • Worksheets
  • Income
  • Inspections
  • Issues
  • Repairs
  • Expenses
Inspection
This feature helps you schedule repair inspections to be made on your assets from time to time and easily fast track by;

  • Add Templates
  • Do Inspections
  • Capture Image evidence
  • Register Issues
Customer
This gives you a detailed list of all your customers with their respective details;

  • Name
  • Contact
  • Address
  • Cumulative Payment
  • Unpaid Invoice balances
Check In and Check Out
This module keeps record of when your asset leaves your premises to the time it checks back.

  • Asset Name
  • Name of Customer who hired the asset
  • Planned check in date
  • Actual check in date
  • Check In condition
  • Added by
Inventory Management
This feature is your “virtual store” where you have a comprehensive list of all your assets’ spare parts in your store clearly indicative of the parts;
  • Name
  • Part number
  • Inventory type
  • Unit measure
  • Unit cost
  • Quantity available in store
  • Image of part
Trip Management
This feature keeps tabs on each asset’s trip to the job site and the cost incurred in this trip.

  • Asset name
  • Date
  • Client
  • Distance
  • Cost
  • Destination
.
Finance
This feature has all the financial data generated from managing your assets and with these results from this module, administrative decisions are made.
  • Accounts
  • Income
  • Expenses
  • Invoices
  • Ledger
  • Expense Category
Reports
The system generates reports according to the data it captures. These reports can also be extracted and used for documenting purposes.

  • Name of asset
  • Department
  • Total Income
  • Total Expenses
  • Number of Assignments
  • Issues per asset
