    About EAMS

    EAMS is an Equipment Asset Management System that was developed for equipment and mobile asset owners to easily track and monitor performance, maintenance and location in real-time. Asset Management can be difficult! Especially tracking income, expenses and repairs done for each asset you have on your fleet on a daily basis. Our product is intended to serve all markets across the globe for companies with a fleet of equipment in rental, leasing, Construction and Civil Engineering, Industrial, government, mining and Dealerships
